About the Game

Blend in, hide among the crowd, and achieve your objectives without being spotted!

Stealth Syndicate is an exhilarating party game for 2 to 10 players, where strategy, speed, and stealth come together. Play locally or online and outsmart your opponents to claim victory!

https://store.steampowered.com/app/3047320/Stealth_Syndicate

Game Modes

Race

Be the first to cross the finish line while eliminating characters you think are players.

Battle Royale

Be the last survivor in a shrinking zone.

Target

Spot and kill your target; if you fail, you're eliminated.

Green Light

Like the race, but advance when the lights are off.

Zombie

Activate computers while avoiding increasingly numerous zombies.

Domination

Team-based gameplay where players capture zones.

Gold

When the lights go out, touch a statue to win.
